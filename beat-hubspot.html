Beating HubSpot in a Competitive Deal

HubSpot wins on ease and free tiers. You win when the deal gets complex and costs start compounding. Here is how to expose the gap before they sign.

Where you win against HubSpot
Enterprise pipeline depthAdvanced forecastingScales without price shockBetter sales-side reportingComplex deal management
Top objections & how to handle them
“HubSpot is free to start.”
Show TCO at their contact volume and seat count. Run the 18-month cost model live on the call — HubSpot pricing compounds fast and most buyers do not see it coming until renewal.
“Our marketing team loves HubSpot.”
Separate the conversation. Ask the VP Sales what pipeline reporting they cannot get today. Marketing loving a tool does not mean sales is winning with it.
“We are already using HubSpot CRM.”
Focus on the sales layer, not replacement. What deal complexity does your solution handle that HubSpot cannot? Start there and expand.
Battlecard: key moves in the deal
1Pull the HubSpot renewal date early — deals close fastest when contract timing creates natural urgency.
2Run a cost model live: contacts x tiers + sequence limits + reporting add-ons = a surprising total.
3Ask: "What reports can you not run today in HubSpot?" The gap list becomes your pitch.
4Find a win against HubSpot in a similar-size company in their vertical — same size, same pain point.
