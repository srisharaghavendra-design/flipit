Common questions

What industries does FlipIt cover?
HR Tech, CRM, ITSM, Cyber Insurance, Marketing Automation, and Cloud Infrastructure. More industries added weekly. If your competitor isn't in the database yet, FlipIt still generates a plan using AI — it just gets sharper with curated intel.
How is this different from Crayon or Klue?
Crayon and Klue track what competitors are doing. FlipIt tells you what to do right now in a deal you're losing. It's deal-moment intelligence, not general competitive monitoring. And it costs a fraction of the price.
Is my deal data stored anywhere?
During beta, no deal data is stored. Your inputs go directly to Claude AI and are not saved on our servers. Full privacy policy coming with paid plans.
Can I add my own competitors?
Custom competitor databases are coming in the Team plan. In the meantime, email us at early@flipit.fyi and we'll add your specific competitors manually.
