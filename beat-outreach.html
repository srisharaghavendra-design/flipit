Beating Outreach in a Competitive Deal

Outreach wins on market leadership and Salesforce depth. You win on rep simplicity, ramp speed, and real adoption. Here is the exact playbook.

Where you win against Outreach

Top objections & how to handle them
“Reps are already trained on Outreach.”
Training is a switching cost, not a lock-in. Ask: what is the ramp time for a new rep on Outreach today? That number becomes your onboarding comparison and time-to-productivity story.
“Outreach has the deepest Salesforce integration.”
Agree and pivot: ask what they actually use from that integration daily vs what they pay for. Most orgs use less than 20% of Outreach features — the rest is shelf cost they have rationalized.
“Procurement will not approve a new vendor.”
Get a champion at VP Sales level. Frame it as a pilot — 10 reps, 90 days, compare pipeline contribution. Low-risk entry that procurement cannot easily block.
Battlecard: key moves in the deal
1Ask sequence completion rate — most Outreach orgs are below 50%. That gap is your entire value story for rep adoption.
2Ask rep satisfaction score directly. Outreach is management-layer loved and rep-layer resented. Surface that tension.
3Pilot framing: 10 reps, 90 days, compare pipeline contribution — lowers risk and speeds the decision.
4Get the renewal date early. Displacement deals close fastest when contract timing creates natural urgency.
